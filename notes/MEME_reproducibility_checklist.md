## Interpretation Checkpoints

**Key results for manuscript accuracy:**

1. **CX3C motif:** MEME did not identify episodic diversifying selection within the CX3C motif (residues 182-186 in A2 numbering) in either RSV subtype.

2. **Broader CCR:** MEME identified one non-CX3C CCR site per subtype:
   - RSV-A: position 178 in alignment (A2 residue 178), p = 0.0438
   - RSV-B: position 181 in alignment (A2 residue 164), p = 0.00053

3. **Mucin domain:** Most MEME sites fall in the C-terminal mucin-like region (residues 199-298 in A2 numbering).

4. **FEL comparison:** Of the MEME-positive sites, some are also FEL-positive (mucin-domain sites) but the broader-CCR MEME sites are not FEL-positive.

## Limitations

- MEME uses unadjusted p-value thresholds (p <= 0.05). Individual sites are screening-level calls, not FDR-controlled discoveries.
- MEME detects episodic diversifying selection on specific branches. The branch sparsity (number of branches under selection) is reported in the parsed tables.
- Tree uncertainty, recombination sensitivity, and duplicate-sequence effects were not formally tested.
- Alignment uncertainty was not formally propagated, though the codon alignment was translation-aware to reduce errors.
