2026-07-04  ADDENDUM: properly-powered region-level selection analysis
================================================================================
Motivation:
- The pre-registered PRIMARY test (site-count enrichment) is underpowered: with
  only 3-6 FEL-significant sites per subtype, "0 in CCR" gives p=1.0 almost
  automatically. A referee will (rightly) call the negative result uninformative
  on its own. This addendum adds a companion test that uses ALL codons.

Method (code/region_selection_analysis.py):
- Metric: per-codon dN-dS = FEL beta - alpha (negative = purifying/constraint,
  positive = diversifying). No significance threshold involved.
- Regions (A2 numbering, locked 2026-07-04, mutually exclusive partition):
  Nterm 1-156, CCR 157-198, Mucin 199-298; CX3C 182-186 nested in CCR (reported
  separately, not a partition cell); ON1-duplication codons with no A2 residue
  labelled 'insertion' (sensitivity only).
- Stats (nonparametric, per user decision): Kruskal-Wallis omnibus across
  Nterm/CCR/Mucin, then directional Mann-Whitney U (CCR<Mucin, CCR<non-CCR,
  CX3C<Mucin) with rank-biserial effect size (rbc). Fisher on purifying-site
  fraction kept as a secondary count-based check.

Results:
  RSV-A:  KW H=7.19, df=2, p=0.0275, eps^2=0.018 (regions differ).
          CCR<Mucin: p=0.00662, rbc=-0.264 (CCR more purifying; medium effect).
          CCR<non-CCR: p=0.00939, rbc=-0.224.
          CX3C<Mucin: p=0.0796, rbc=-0.376 (ns; only 5 codons, low power).
  RSV-B:  KW H=2.76, df=2, p=0.252 (NOT significant).
          CCR<Mucin: p=0.0742, rbc=-0.154 (same direction, trend only).
          CCR<non-CCR: p=0.0561 (borderline). CX3C<Mucin: p=0.265.

Honest interpretation (Discussion, not Results):
- The powered analysis SUPPORTS the mechanistic story in RSV-A: the CCR is under
  significantly stronger purifying selection than the mucin domain / rest of the
  protein, with a medium effect size. In RSV-B the same direction holds but does
  not reach significance (trend). So the constraint-on-CCR claim is subtype-
  dependent in strength: clear in A, suggestive in B. Report both; do NOT
  overstate B.
- This is consistent with (and mechanistically explains) the primary negative
  result: positive selection is not enriched in the CCR BECAUSE the CCR is
  purifying-constrained, while diversifying selection concentrates in the mucin
  flanks.
